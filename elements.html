A bit About Me...

Since my first lines of code, I have been captivated by the endless possibilities that coding enables. In many ways, I now see coding as a practical art form - an application where beautiful design meets clever functions. This fusion of art and practicality is why I love to code.

I am seeking opportunities to connect this passion to a greater purpose. Creating something of value via programming is my driving force. Smart software solutions can save time, prevent redundant tasks, or benefit people’s lives in any multitude of ways – this is something I am always eager to pursue.

Some of my favorite subjects include: Astronomy, Natural Science, Finance, Technology and Business. My entrepreneurial interest has led me to create an Etsy shop that sells photos I have taken while traveling. I also occasionally blog about stock picks on TradingView. I also enjoy hiking, surfing, reading, making (and drinking) kombucha, and training at the gym.
